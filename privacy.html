Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

At Fortune AI Labs, your privacy and digital security are our absolute highest priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our predictive services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with personalized destiny mapping, our system requires minimal input. We collect only the data you explicitly provide in the input fields, which may include:

  • Your First and/or Last Name
  • The Name of a Secondary Individual (e.g., a "Crush" or Partner)
  • Your Age

We also collect non-identifiable, aggregated usage data, such as your general geographical region (city/country level) to fuel our Global Alignment Ticker, and your device's basic browser capabilities to ensure the site renders correctly.

2. Data Encryption and Zero-Storage Architecture

We operate on a strict Zero-Storage architecture designed to protect your most sensitive emotional queries. When you input names into our system, the text is immediately subjected to secure cryptographic hashing locally on your device. This allows our AI to process the "vibrational footprint" of the text without transmitting raw, unencrypted letters to our central databases.

The names you enter are temporarily held in the browser's local RAM for the exact 3.5 seconds required for the synaptic analysis to complete. Once your result is rendered on your screen, the input data is entirely wiped. We do not maintain databases of the names you search.

3. Use of Information

The information processed by Fortune AI is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To generate and deliver your personalized compatibility and fortune scores.
  • To enforce daily usage limits (managed locally via your browser's Local Storage).
  • To monitor global server loads and optimize our neural networking algorithms.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We absolutely do not sell, rent, or share any personal queries, names, or fortunes with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or external corporate entities. Your destiny is yours alone. We do not use tracking pixels to follow you across the internet based on the fortunes you receive.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Fortune AI utilizes your browser's Local Storage mechanism rather than traditional tracking cookies. This is solely used to maintain your daily allowance of fortunes (e.g., the 5-fortune limit). This data does not track your identity and remains on your physical device.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time as our AI models evolve. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

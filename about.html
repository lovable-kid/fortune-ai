The Technology

At the core of Fortune AI is our proprietary Synaptic Vibe-Sync framework. Traditional fortune-telling relies on generalized statements. The result is a dynamic compatibility and destiny score that reflects the true, underlying energetic currents of your current life phase. Our algorithms are constantly learning, updating their predictive matrices based on global emotional shifts and macro-environmental factors.
